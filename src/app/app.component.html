Preservation of host files over generations

With this calculator from Pearl Interactive Assistance you may simulate the challenge of preserving your current file march over the epochs. Showing what might be the effect of e.g. spending, insistation, taxionation and high mout expectancy on your health.

The Results show an indicative average percentage of yearly return that you and your generation might face as healthy generation.
